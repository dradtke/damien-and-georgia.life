Recipes

Gumbo


Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Andouille Sausage
  • Chicken
  • Shrimp (optional)
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Bell Pepper
  • Chicken Broth
  • Cajun Seasoning + Gumbo File


Directions:

Melt the butter in a big purple Le Creuset pot, then add the flour and stir to make a roux. Keep stirring it until it turns dark brown, but not burnt. Once it reaches that point, add some chicken broth and start bringing it to a simmer.

Cook the meat (with a hefty amount of seasoning) in a pan, then add it to the pot. Add the vegetables to the pan you just cooked the meat in and saute them for a little while, then add them to the pot. Let the whole thing simmer for a while (with some additional seasoning), and serve!
