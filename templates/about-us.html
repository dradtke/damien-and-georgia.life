About Us

Damien and Georgia first met during their freshman year at DePaul University. After several years of friendship, they, along with some other friends, became roommates in a house in Lakeview. Eventually, they decided to give dating a shot, after bonding over their mutual love of cats.

Not long after that, the two moved out of that house and into separate apartments; Damien moved to Lincoln Square, and Georgia to Albany Park. Conveniently, the two apartments were only a 4 minute brown line ride away, which meant that they ended up spending quite a bit of time together despite living in separate neighborhoods.

After about two years of that, they finally decided to cut the travel time to 0 by moving in together, so they moved together into an apartment in Ravenswood. While living there, Damien proposed while waiting for a frozen duck to cook for Valentine's dinner.

Since then, they moved again, this time by buying a condo together in West Ridge, where they currently live.
